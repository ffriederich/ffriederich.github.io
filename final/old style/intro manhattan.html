Ingredients

  • 1.75 liter bottle of vodka
  • 2 oz. wisky
  • 0.25 oz. scotch
  • 0.50 oz. sweet vermouth
  • 2-3 dash bitters

Directions

  1. Add the wisky, sweet vermouth and bitters.
  2. Stir it with ice and pour into glass.
  3. Garnish with a cherry.
