Manhattan

Materials

  • Old fashiones class crown style

  • Manhattan
  • Tom Colins
  • Piña Colada
  • Dirty Martini

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ liter bottle of vodka

  • 2 oz. wisky

  • 0 ¼ oz. scotch

  • 0 ½ oz. sweet vermouth

  • 2-3 dash bitters

Directions

  1. Add the wisky, sweet vermouth and bitters.

  2. Stir it with ice and pour into glass.

  3. Garnish with a cherry.
