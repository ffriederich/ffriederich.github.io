Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Pineapple juice
  • 1.5 oz of White rum
  • 1.5 oz of Coconut cream
  • Pineapple slice

Directions

  1. Mix the pineapple juice adn the coconut rum in the blender with ice.
  2. Mix until crushed ice is smooth.
  3. Serve the white rum on the tall glass and then add the mix.
  4. Garnish and serve.
