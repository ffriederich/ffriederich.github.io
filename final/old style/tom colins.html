Ingredients

  • 2 oz. dry gin
  • ice cubes
  • 2 oz. lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (gomme) syrup
  • 2-3 dash bitters
  • soda water
  • slice of lemon
  • 1 colored cherry

Directions

  1. Place ample ice in large glass.
  2. Add gin, lemon juice and syrup.
  3. Top up with soda water and stir well.
  4. Serve with lemon slice, cherry and a straw.
