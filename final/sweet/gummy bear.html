Giant Gummy Bear

Materials

  • A large bowl

  • Plastic wrap

  • Watermelon Keg
  • Giant Vodka Gummy Bears
  • Skittles Vodkag

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle of Vodka

  • 1 bottle of Peach Schnapps

  • 1 bottle of coconut rum

  • Giant gummy bear

Directions

  1. Place the gummy bear into a the bowl.

  2. Pour all the vodka, peach schnapps, and coconut rum into the bowl to cover the gummy bear completely.

  3. Cover the bowl in plastic wrap. Place the covered bowl in the refrigerator. Allow the gummy bear to infuse for 2 days.

  4. Remove the gummy bear from the bowl. The gummy bear will have absorbed most, if not all of the vodka.
