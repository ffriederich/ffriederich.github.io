Vodka Skiletts

Materials

  • A funnel

  • 5 8½ ounce flasks or bottles

  • Bowls for separating the Skittles into flavors

  • A measuring cup

  • Cheesecloth and coffee filters

  • Watermelon Keg
  • Giant Vodka Gummy Bears
  • Skittles Vodkag

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ liter bottle of vodka

  • 1 1 pound bag of Skittles

Directions

  1. Start by simply separating the Skittles into flavors.

  2. Now put your Skittles into 5 bottles.

  3. Now add the 7 ounces of vodka to each bottle. When this is done, all five bottles will have a pile of Skittles soaking in the vodka.

  4. Now your infusion bottles all have Skittles in the bottom. Give each bottle a good shake – the more, the better. The candy coating will start to rub off and color the vodka, but the white insides of the Skittles still have a lot of dissolving to do.

  5. the quickest and most thorough way to strain these is through: 2 layers of cheese cloth inside a coffee filter. Poured one of the infusions into my strainer setup.In the end, you’ll have about 8 ounces of Skittle infused vodka.

  6. Repeat this process for each flavor.
