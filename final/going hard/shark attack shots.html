Shark Attack Shots

Materials

  • 1 shacker/strainer with ice

  • 1 double shot class

  • 1 rocks glasss

  • Spoon

  • Shark Attack Shots
  • Jäger Bombs
  • Jungle Juice
  • Kamikaze Shots

Ingredients

  • splash blue curacao

  • 1 oz lemon/lime soda

  • 1 oz. grenadine

  • 1 oz. cherry vodka

  • 1 oz. tequila

Directions

  1. Pour 1oz of Grenadine and 1oz of Cherry Flavored Vodka into the shot glass.

  2. Add 1oz of tequila and a slpash of blue curacao of Shaker/Strainer.

  3. Throw in 3oz of your favorite Lemon/Lime soda into your Shaker Strainer.

  4. Remember, you are dealing with a carbonated mix. DO NOT SHAKE!!! Instead, stir the contents with your spoon.

  5. Set aside your Shaker/Strainer and place your Rocks Glass on top of your Shot Glass and turn it upside.

  6. Now that your "blood" is in place, time to pour in the "water". Pour in the contents of the Shaker/Strainer on TOP of the shot glass and let it run down the glass.
