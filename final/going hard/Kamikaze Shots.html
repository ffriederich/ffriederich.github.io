Kamikaze Shots

Materials

  • 1 shacker/strainer with ice

  • 1 double shot class

  • Shark Attack Shots
  • Jäger Bombs
  • Jungle Juice
  • Kamikaze Shots

Ingredients

  • Vodka

  • Triple sec

  • Lime juice

Directions

  1. Pour ½ shot of vodka, ¼ shotof triple sec, and ¼ shot lime juice in to the shacker with ice. Shake it.

  2. Pour it in the double shot glass.
