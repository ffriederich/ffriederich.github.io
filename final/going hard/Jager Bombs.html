Jäger Bombs

Materials

  • 1 pub glass

  • 1 shot class

  • Shark Attack Shots
  • Jäger Bombs
  • Jumgle Juice
  • Kamikaze Shots

Ingredients

  • ½ oz Jägermeister herbal liqueur

  • ½ can Red Bull energy drink

Directions

  1. Pour one shot of jäger.

  2. Pour the 1/2 can of red bull in the pub glass.

  3. Drop a shot glass filled with Jägermeister into a pub glass containing Red Bull.
