Jungle Juice

Materials

  • Knife

  • Big container

  • Shark Attack Shots
  • Jäger Bombs
  • Jungle Juice
  • Kamikaze Shots

Ingredients

  • 10 apples

  • 10 oranges

  • 4 mangos

  • 2 pineapples

  • 1 L Everclear® alcohol

  • 5 ¼ L(3 1 ¾L bottles) vodka

  • 1 bottle peach schnapps

  • 1 pint Bacardi® 151 rum

  • 1 bottle 99 Apples® apple schnapps

  • 10 L Sprite® soda

  • 1 L Sunny Delight® orange juice

  • 1 L triple sec

  • 1 ¾ Lbottle gin

  • 1 bottle DeKuyper® Sour Apple Pucker schnapps

  • 4 bottles Boone's Farm® Strawberry Hill wine

  • 8 L Hawaiian Punch®

  • 2 containers orange juice concentrate

Directions

  1. Cut all the frut into slices and put it in the big container.

  2. Pour everything ells in the big container.

  3. Allow the fruit to soak in all the alcohol for about 4 - 12 hours. Add all the juice and let sit overnight. Serve the next day.
