Preparation

Whisk oil, lemon juice, mustard, garlic, and lemon peel in small bowl to bend. Season dressing with salt and pepper. Cook penne in a large pot of boiling salted water until tender but still firm to bit. Drain. Rinde pasta with cold water to cool quickly and drain again. Transfer pasta to a large bowl. Add tomatoes, bell pepper, feta cheese. and green onions. Pour dressing over and toss to coat. Season to teast with salt and pepper.
