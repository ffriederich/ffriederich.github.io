Cramberry, Pomegranate, and Lemon Relish

Ingredeints

  • 3 1/2 cups cranberries (about 14 ounces)

  • 1 cup pure pomegranate juice

  • 3/4 cup sugar

  • 1/4 cup fresh Meyer lemon juice or regular lemon juice

  • 2 tablespoons finely grated Meyer lemon peel or regular lemon peel

  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

    • Preparation

    Combine first 5 ingredients in a large saucepan. Stir over medium-heigh heat until sugar dissolve. Bring to boil. reduce heat to medium; simmer until berries begin to burst, stirring often, about 10 minutes. tranfers to small bowl. Stir in parsley. Cover and chill until cold. DO AHEAD: Can be made 3 days ahead. Keep chilled.
